Example: Lead Qualification Call

This assistant evaluates calls and assigns a score based on what the customer says during the conversation.

Goal

Automatically decide whether a call is Qualified or Not Qualified.

How to Define Success Criteria ?

WHAT YOU WANT TO CHECK COMPARATOR VALUE POINTS
Interested Lead = Yes 40
Education Loan Required = true 30
Follow-up Booked = Booked 30

Allowed Values

  • Yes, No
  • true, false
  • Interested, Booked
  • 1, 0

Avoid These

  • Long sentences
  • Multiple conditions in one value
  • Complex logic (keep it simple)

Rule of Thumb: If the AI can clearly detect it from the call, it is a good success criteria.
